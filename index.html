WHOTYPE

[ ho͞o - tīp ]


NOUN: (Whotype) is a unique social experience for exploring personality (the self). Whotype visualizes personality patterns and motivations to help explore the most important personal and professional relationships. Whotype is the Power of Personality.

VERB: (Whotyped) is to understand the unique personality patterns and quirks of a person, group, or company. To be Whotyped is to be understood.

LET'S GET PERSONAL

Whotype is all about understanding important relationships, the ones that truly matter. Your closest family and friends. Understanding others that we care about is the key to harmony in our own life. Whotype helps explore the dynamics of each amazing relationship you have so that you can laugh more, stress less, and truly connect with one another.

LETS GET REAL

If you’re single, find someone who balances you into your best self. Relationships often boil down to money, sex, and communication and these three are often not equally important to one another, so Whotype helps in visualizing and understanding what motivates the ones we love so that we can create fulfilling, lasting, and loving relationships.

LET’S GET PROFESSIONAL

Whotype supports you in your advancement of understanding how motivations fit with those around you, and unveil your root needs for fulfillment within. Motivations are what make us do what we do. Understanding our colleagues and professional connections creates workplace harmony which is where something amazing is produced together.

COMPANIES HAVE A PERSONALITY TOO!

From Startups to Industry Leaders, Whotype is harmony at work, especially in HR. Whotype helps leading leading companies to better understand any candidate and process the spectrum of team dynamics. Measure teams, working groups, and the company personality as a whole. Develop, advance, and promote true workplace harmony that’s encouraged and effective and then help teams manage and motivate one another toward great things.

Coupler

Explore relationships that require deep focus, attention, and care-taking. Explore the dynamics of how to best work and best agree with any person you know.

Lifegraph

Your sphere of influence, visualized. Sort the importance and influence of your relationships and map how your world connects with you. The closer they are to you, the more you’ll see about them in your feed as well!

Harmonizer

Relationships are personalities at work. Harmonizer helps understand the possible pattern outputs that are likely to come from any set of group dynamics be it one on one or teams - to companies. Harmonizer helps you understand the company you keep.

Company Pages

Explore how you fit in your company, what personality lives within teams, and with specific co-workers. Using WhoType company pages, workplace dynamics are visualized, helping to understand and manage culture and workplace harmony.

Feed

Whotype uses the lifegraph to create a feed about what really matters most to you. The Whotype feed brings the information about the people you care most about to you, and relates it to your personality.

Search

Find anything about anyone. Quirks, personality type, patterns, behaviors, stress indicators, turn-ons, and core motivations. Search any quality in people you can think of.
