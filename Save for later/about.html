ABOUT

Who we are

Whotype is a new kind of social, purpose-built by software engineers, creatives/designers, and social engineers in Austin, Texas and designed to explore our personalities, motivations, and connectedness in our most important personal or professional relationships. Whotype is for any person, group, team, and also for human resources within leading companies. Whotype provides a "truly connected" mathematical model for exploring people and their QuirksTM.

LET'S GET PERSONAL

Whotype is all about understanding important relationships, the ones that truly matter. Your closest family and friends. Understanding others that we care about is the key to harmony in our own life. Whotype helps explore the dynamics of each amazing relationship you have so that you can laugh more, stress less, and truly connect with one another.

LETS GET REAL

If you’re single, find someone who balances you into your best self. Relationships often boil down to money, sex, and communication and these three are often not equally important to one another, so Whotype helps in visualizing and understanding what motivates the ones we love so that we can create fulfilling, lasting, and loving relationships.

LET’S GET PROFESSIONAL

Whotype supports you in your advancement of understanding how motivations fit with those around you, and unveil your root needs for fulfillment within. Motivations are what make us do what we do. Understanding our colleagues and professional connections creates workplace harmony which is where something amazing is produced together.

COMPANIES HAVE A PERSONALITY TOO!

From Startups to Industry Leaders, Whotype is harmony at work, especially in HR. Whotype helps leading leading companies to better understand any candidate and process the spectrum of team dynamics. Measure teams, working groups, and the company personality as a whole. Develop, advance, and promote true workplace harmony that’s encouraged and effective and then help teams manage and motivate one another toward great things.
