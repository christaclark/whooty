FEATURES

Coupler

Explore relationships that require deep focus, attention, and care-taking. Explore the dynamics of how to best work and best agree with any person you know.

Lifegraph

Your sphere of influence, visualized. Sort the importance and influence of your relationships and map how your world connects with you. The closer they are to you, the more you’ll see about them in your feed as well!

Harmonizer

Relationships are personalities at work. Harmonizer helps understand the possible pattern outputs that are likely to come from any set of group dynamics be it one on one or teams - to companies. Harmonizer helps you understand the company you keep.

Company Pages

Explore how you fit in your company, what personality lives within teams, and with specific co-workers. Using WhoType company pages, workplace dynamics are visualized, helping to understand and manage culture and workplace harmony.

Feed

Whotype uses the lifegraph to create a feed about what really matters most to you. The Whotype feed brings the information about the people you care most about to you, and relates it to your personality.

Search

Find anything about anyone. Quirks, personality type, patterns, behaviors, stress indicators, turn-ons, and core motivations. Search any quality in people you can think of.
